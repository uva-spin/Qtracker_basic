pure classification. By contrast, ResNet had best residual results, indicating
it predicts hits closest to the actual signal track even if the predictions are 
still wrong. Based on this, it's hard to quantify which is better between ResNet

incorrectly classified results but not by how far off the wrong predictions are.

as well.
